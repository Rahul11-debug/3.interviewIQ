# 🚀 AI Interview IQ

## 📌 Overview

AI Interview IQ is an AI-powered mock interview platform that helps students and job seekers prepare for technical and HR interviews through personalized interview experiences.

Users can upload their resume 📄, select their target role 💼 and experience level 🎯, and participate in AI-driven mock interviews. The platform generates tailored interview questions based on the candidate's profile, evaluates responses, and provides detailed performance reports 📊 with actionable feedback.

---

## ✨ Features

* 📄 Resume-based interview question generation
* 💼 Role-specific and experience-based interview customization
* 🤖 AI-generated technical and behavioral questions
* 🎙️ Voice-based interview responses
* ⌨️ Text-based interview responses
* ⚡ Real-time AI evaluation and feedback
* 📊 Detailed performance scoring and analysis
* 📥 Downloadable interview performance reports
* 📚 Interview history tracking
* 🔐 Firebase Authentication (Email & Google Sign-In)
* 💳 Credit-based system with Razorpay integration
* 🎨 Responsive and modern user interface

## 🔄 Workflow

### 1️⃣ Authentication

Users sign up or log in securely using Firebase Authentication.

### 2️⃣ Resume Upload

Upload your resume 📄 for personalized interview preparation.

### 3️⃣ Select Preferences

Choose your target role 💼 and experience level 🎯.

### 4️⃣ AI Interview Generation

OpenRouter AI analyzes your profile and generates relevant interview questions 🤖.

### 5️⃣ Answer Questions

Respond through:

* 🎙️ Voice
* ⌨️ Text

### 6️⃣ AI Evaluation

The AI evaluates responses based on:

* 🧠 Technical Knowledge
* 🗣️ Communication Skills
* 🔍 Problem Solving Ability
* 💪 Confidence
* ⭐ Overall Performance

### 7️⃣ Performance Report

Receive a comprehensive report including:

* 📈 Performance Score
* ✅ Strengths
* ⚠️ Areas for Improvement
* 💡 Personalized Suggestions

### 8️⃣ Download Report

Download and print your interview performance report 📥🖨️ for future reference.
